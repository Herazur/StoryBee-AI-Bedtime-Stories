# Privacy Policy for StoryBee: AI Bedtime Stories

**Last Updated:** November 16, 2024

## Overview
StoryBee ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Information:** Email address (for login and authentication)
- **Child Information:** Child's name and age (used only for story personalization)
- **User Preferences:** Story themes, language preferences, and personalization settings

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version
- **Usage Data:** App usage statistics, story generation frequency
- **Analytics Data:** Anonymous usage patterns to improve our service

### Story Content
- **Generated Stories:** Stories created through our AI are stored locally on your device and in your secure user account
- **User Stories:** Your stories are private and only accessible by you

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Generate personalized bedtime stories for your child
- Provide and maintain app functionality
- Authenticate your account and secure access
- Improve our AI story generation service
- Send important updates about your account or subscription
- Analyze app performance and user experience

## Data Storage and Security

- **Firebase Authentication:** Secure authentication via Google Firebase
- **Cloud Firestore:** User data stored in secure, encrypted Firebase databases
- **Local Storage:** Stories cached locally on your device using Hive
- **Encryption:** All data transmission uses industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS)

## Children's Privacy

StoryBee is designed for parents to create stories for their children. We do NOT:
- Collect personal information directly from children
- Allow children to create accounts
- Share children's names or data with third parties for marketing
- Store children's data beyond what's necessary for story personalization

**COPPA Compliance:** We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). Children's names are used solely for story personalization and are not shared with third parties.

## User Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access:** View all data we have about you
- **Update:** Modify your account information and preferences
- **Delete:** Request deletion of your account and all associated data
- **Export:** Download your stories and data
- **Opt-Out:** Disable analytics and marketing communications

### Data Deletion
To delete your account and all data:
1. Open StoryBee app → Profile → Account Settings
2. Select "Delete Account"
3. Or email us at: support@herazur.com

All data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts:** Data retained while account is active
- **Inactive Accounts:** Data retained for 2 years, then automatically deleted
- **Deleted Accounts:** Data permanently deleted within 30 days
- **Stories:** Deleted immediately when you delete a story

## Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal tracking technologies:
- **Essential Cookies:** Required for authentication and app functionality
- **Analytics:** Anonymous usage statistics (can be opted out)
- **Advertising:** AdMob cookies for free users (can be limited via device settings)
